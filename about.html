Our Story

Crafting unforgettable scent experiences since 2010

Welcome to Heagrance

Founded in 2025 by master perfumer Rainer Ganteng in the heart of Paris, Heagrance was born from a passion for creating unique fragrances that capture emotions, memories, and stories. What began as a small boutique in the Marais district has grown into an internationally recognized perfume house known for its artisanal approach and commitment to exceptional quality.

Our Philosophy

Sustainable Sourcing

We ethically source the finest natural ingredients from around the world, working directly with farmers and suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability and fair trade practices.

Artisanal Craftsmanship

Each Heagrance fragrance is meticulously crafted by our team of master perfumers, who blend traditional techniques with innovative approaches to create scents that are both timeless and contemporary.

Cruelty-Free

We are proud to be a 100% cruelty-free brand. We never test our products on animals and work only with partners who uphold the same ethical standards.

Eco-Conscious Packaging

Our bottles are designed to be both beautiful and environmentally responsible, using recycled glass and sustainable materials for all packaging.

Our Process

01

Inspiration

Every fragrance begins with a concept or emotion we wish to capture, often inspired by nature, art, travel, or meaningful human experiences.

02

Ingredient Selection

Our perfumers carefully select the highest quality ingredients from our global network of sustainable suppliers, focusing on their unique characteristics and how they will harmonize together.

03

Composition

Creating the perfect balance is an art form. Our perfumers blend top, heart, and base notes to create a fragrance that evolves beautifully over time.

04

Maturation

Each composition matures for several weeks, allowing the ingredients to meld and develop their full complexity.

05

Quality Control

Every batch undergoes rigorous testing to ensure consistency and excellence before being carefully bottled in our signature packaging.
