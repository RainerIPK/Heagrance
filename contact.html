Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused and unopened products in their original packaging. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer complimentary standard shipping on all orders over $100. For orders under $100, a flat shipping rate of $10 applies.

Can I request fragrance samples?

Yes, we offer a sampling program that allows you to experience our fragrances before committing to a full-size bottle. You can order a set of three samples for $15, which is redeemable towards a future full-size purchase.

Are your fragrances cruelty-free?

Absolutely. All Heagrance products are 100% cruelty-free. We never test our products on animals and work only with suppliers who uphold the same ethical standards.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 90 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary based on your location. You can view specific rates at checkout.
