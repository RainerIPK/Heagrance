About Heagrance

Established in 2010, Heagrance is a premium perfume house dedicated to creating extraordinary fragrances that evoke emotions and memories. Founded by master perfumer Elena Granier in Paris, we have grown from a small boutique to an internationally recognized brand while maintaining our commitment to artisanal quality and sustainability.

Each Heagrance perfume is meticulously crafted using the finest ingredients sourced from around the world, combining traditional perfumery techniques with innovative approaches to create distinctive signature scents.

Our Philosophy

Sustainable Sourcing

We ethically source the finest natural ingredients from around the world, working directly with farmers and suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability.

Artisanal Craftsmanship

Each Heagrance fragrance is meticulously crafted by our team of master perfumers, who blend traditional techniques with innovative approaches.

Cruelty-Free

We are proud to be a 100% cruelty-free brand. We never test our products on animals and work only with partners who uphold the same ethical standards.
